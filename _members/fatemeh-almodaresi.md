I studied at Southeast University until 2026 and joined the ARTE Lab in the spring of 2026.
My primary research interests lie in modelling the laws governing light propagation and, building on this, achieving high-precision scene characterisation.
Prior to joining ARTE, my primary research focused on FDTD-based electromagnetic modelling and simulation, as well as the design of light-modulated liquid crystal metasurface devices.
Currently, I am working on a project involving 3D reconstruction using polarised light. I look forward to hearing from you and discussing further!
