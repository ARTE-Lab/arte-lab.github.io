abstract: "We present OpenBench to evaluate how well multimodal large language models perform spatial reasoning in realistic open-world scenes. The benchmark is built from pedestrian-perspective multimodal data with metric 3D cues, and includes question types ranging from qualitative relations to quantitative and kinematic reasoning. Results show that performance gains reported in indoor settings do not transfer well to open-world scenes, highlighting a major gap in grounded spatial intelligence."
